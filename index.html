Hi I'm Daniel: Emergency Care Practitioner (Paramedic) and lecturer. I teach the next generation of paramedics at Nelson Mandela University. My passion lies in transforming emergency care education through AI and game-based learning - finding new ways to prepare healthcare professionals for the challenges they'll face in the field.

As an Emergency Care Practitioner registered with HPCSA, I've seen firsthand how technology can enhance medical training. Now, while pursuing my Masters in healthcare education technology, I'm exploring ways to merge traditional clinical education with digital innovation.

I share updates about my work through my newsletter. You can also find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, and ResearchGate.
